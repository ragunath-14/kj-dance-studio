**Last Updated: May 20, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how our application collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our WhatsApp communication services for Dance Studio.

* **Phone Numbers:** We collect your WhatsApp phone number solely to send automated notifications, updates, and class alerts regarding our dance programs.
* **Chat Metadata:** We handle automated message delivery status reports provided by the Meta WhatsApp Cloud API.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information strictly to:
* Send transactional service notifications and utility updates.
* Respond to user-initiated customer support queries.

### 3. Data Security & Third Parties

* We do not sell or trade your personal information or phone numbers.

### 4. User Rights & Data Deletion
