**Last Updated:** May 30, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our Dance Studio website collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website, register for classes, or interact with our online services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the Site includes:

### Personal Data
Personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and student details (e.g., age or dance level for class placement), which you voluntarily provide to us when you:
* Fill out a contact or registration form.
* Sign up for programs (e.g., Summer Camps, workshops).
* Subscribe to our newsletters or updates via website popups.

### Derivative Data
Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Site, such as your IP address, your browser type, your operating system, your access times, and the pages you have viewed directly before and after accessing the Site.

## 2. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site to:
* Manage student registrations and class schedules.
* Deliver targeted marketing, promotional materials, and special announcements (such as upcoming dance camps or studio schedules).
* Respond to customer service requests and support needs.
* Improve website functionality, layouts, and interactive user interface components.

## 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and other tracking technologies on the Site to help customize the Site and improve your experience. 

* **Functional Cookies:** These are used to remember user preferences, such as whether a promotional banner or seasonal program popup has already been dismissed, ensuring a seamless user experience.
* **Analytics Cookies:** We may use third-party analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to monitor and analyze web traffic and understand user behavior.

You can choose to remove or reject cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may affect the availability and functionality of the Site.

Because our studio offers dance classes for children under the age of 13, we take extra care regarding privacy. Any information collected regarding a minor (such as registration details for children's camps) must be explicitly provided by a parent or legal guardian. We do not intentionally target or collect data directly from children under 13.
